Short Bio


    I am a second-year Ph.D. student at State Univeristy of New York at Buffalo (UB), advised by Prof. Junsong Yuan. Before that, I got B.Eng. from Tianjin University, where I continued one-year M.Eng. study and left for UB before finishing the degree. I received the Best CSE First Year Achiever Award from UB. My current research interest lies in object-centric video analysis including detection, segmentation, and tracking.
