I obtained the MSc. degree from Tianjin University at January 2020. Before that, I received the B.Eng. degree from Southwest University in 2017. Previously I was a research intern at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI), Abu Dhabi, UAE. Now I am a research intern at MEGVII. My research interests include: Computer Vision, Object Detection, Human Object Interaction and Instance Segmentation.
