Short Bio



    I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at CSE department, State University of New York at Buffalo (UB), advised by Prof. Junsong Yuan. Before that, I got B.Eng. from Tianjin University, where I continued one-year M.Eng. study and left for UB before finishing the degree. I received the Best CSE First Year Achiever Award from UB. My previous research experience is object-centric video analysis including detection, segmentation, and tracking; object understanding in vision and language.
