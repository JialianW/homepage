Short Bio


    I am a second-year Ph.D. student at State Univeristy of New York at Buffalo (UB), advised by Prof. Junsong Yuan. Before that, I studied towards M.Eng. at Tianjin University during 2018-2019 and left for UB in 2019 before finishing my degree. I received B.Eng. from Tianjin University in 2018. I had a great research time at Horizon Robotics working with Dr. Yu Wang and Dr. Qian Zhang. My current research interest is object-centric video analysis including detection, segmentatoin, and tracking.
