I started this page for sharing my favorite musics. Hope you can enjoy!


Kotaro Oshio

Kotaro is my favorite fingerstyle guitar player. His covered songs are mostly very emotional. I used to practice his songs in high school but was only able to perform two easiest songs of his as playing fingerstyle guitar is not an easy thing. The second video below (covered by Sungha Jung) is the song 'Fight' which is the well-known hardest song in fingerstyle guitar. But that's pretty cool, isn't it?

Tommy Emmanuel

Tommy is an Australian fingerstyle guitar player. His covered songs are mostly upbeat, while below there are two gentle ones that I like most.

The Beatles

I literally love every song of The Beatles!! The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. They are regarded as the most influential band of all time.

Jakob Dylan

Jakob is an American country-style songwriter who seems to be rarely known by people, but you must know his father, Bob Dylan. I grew up with the "Seeing Things" album and still listen it today. This talented man wrote so many touching songs that definitely deserve much more credits.
